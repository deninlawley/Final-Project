TYPES OF WATCHES

Watches designed for the deep seas. Usually features over 200M of water resistance, a unidiretional bezel with a minute track, and heavy amounts of luminesent paint.

Used to time events, these watches feature a stop watch function and often have a tachymeter to determine speed.

Simple but effective, these watches typically only show the time and sometimes the date. Rarely they can be seen with a bidirectional compass bezel.

Unlike most categories of watches, pilot watches don't have set features. Some may be time only whereas some may feature GMT bezels, timing bezels, sliderule bezels, and even chronographs.
